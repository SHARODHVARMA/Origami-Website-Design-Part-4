Interesting Facts about Camel:-

1. Camel's ears are furry.

2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet.

3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear:-

1. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.

2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

3. There are about 2,000 speciality bear making stores in America! This does not count stores like Build-A-Bear.

Interesting Facts about chameleon:-

1. THEIR FEET WORK LIKE SALAD TONGS.

2. ALMOST HALF OF ALL KNOWN SPECIES LIVE IN MADAGASCAR.

3. THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon:-

1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.

2. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.

3. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.

Interesting Facts about Panda:-

1. They have great camouflage for their environment.

2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.

3. They spend a lot of their day eating.

Interesting Facts about Cicada:-

1. There are around 3,000 cicada species, according to National Geographic.

2. They vary in size from 0.75 to 2.25 in (2.2 to 5.5 cm) long.

3. Their wings are transparent and can seem rainbow-hued when held up to a light source.
